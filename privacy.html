Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

HardBid Consulting collects information you choose to submit through this website so we can review your request, respond to you, and provide contractor bid-review support.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details such as name, email, phone, and company.
  • Project intake details such as project type, bid due date, project size, support requested, notes, and document links.
  • Files or links you upload, such as plans, specs, addenda, scopes, bid forms, and related documents.
  • Basic website/form metadata needed for security, spam prevention, and site operation.

How We Use Information

  • To evaluate and respond to your request.
  • To organize lead and project-intake information.
  • To prepare contractor review support if you approve a scope of work.
  • To protect the website, business email, and submitted documents from abuse or unauthorized access.

Document Authorization

Only submit documents you are authorized to share. Do not upload restricted, confidential, trade-secret, private, or third-party materials unless you have the right to provide them to HardBid for review.

Sharing

HardBid does not sell customer information. Information may be processed through service providers used to operate the website, forms, business email, file storage, calendars, and lead tracking. Access should be limited to business purposes.

Security

HardBid uses reasonable administrative and technical steps to protect submitted information, but no website, email system, or file-transfer method is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of information you submitted by contacting info@hardbidconsulting.com. Some records may be retained when needed for business, legal, security, or accounting purposes.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@hardbidconsulting.com.
