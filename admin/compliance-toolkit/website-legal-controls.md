# Website Legal Controls

## Recommended Public Pattern

Use this structure:

1. Footer links: `Privacy Policy` and `Terms`.
2. Upload form acknowledgement: customer confirms authorization to share documents and accepts Privacy/Terms.
3. Intake disclaimer: HardBid provides contractor decision-support only.
4. Full legal language in `privacy.html` and `terms.html`.

This avoids cluttering the marketing page while still giving the customer a clear notice before submitting information.

## Upload Form Consent

Use short language:

```text
I confirm I am authorized to share these documents and agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms.
```

Keep the checkbox required before form submission.

## Payment Readiness

Before accepting payments online, add or review:

- Payment terms.
- Refund/cancellation policy.
- Scope change policy.
- Success-fee language, if used.
- No-guarantee language for award, bid result, profit, change-order recovery, and quantity accuracy.
- Document confidentiality and customer authorization language.

## Data Controls

- Store customer leads in the HardBid Lead Tracker.
- Keep plan links inside Google Workspace, Netlify, or approved storage only.
- Limit access to Freddy/admin accounts.
- Do not feed customer plans into third-party AI or CRM tools unless the customer has agreed and the tool is approved.
- Remove old test submissions and duplicate test records once automation is verified.
