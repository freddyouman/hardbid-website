What We Do

Practical Support for Better Bid Decisions

From bid review through scope clarification, we provide structured support to help contractors move forward with better information.

Bid Package Review

Review drawings, specifications, addenda, scopes, and bid requirements to help identify missing or unclear information.

Scope Gap Review

Organize possible gaps between drawings, specifications, trade scopes, vendor quotes, and project requirements.

Take-Off Summaries

Prepare organized quantity and take-off summaries with notes, assumptions, and source references for contractor review.

Estimating Support

Support takeoff organization, assumptions, exclusions, bid summaries, and estimate backup without replacing your estimator.
