About the Founder

Freddy R. Leon

Construction Superintendent, Detailer & Estimator · 25+ years in the field

Over 25 years of construction field experience on water treatment plants, commercial projects, and industrial mechanical systems. Freddy has served at the superintendent level on major municipal projects, giving him a field-grounded perspective that most consultants lack.

He founded HardBid Consulting after seeing contractors consistently lose time and money to unclear scopes, missing details, and weak documentation — the gap between winning a bid and protecting a margin. HardBid fills that gap with organized, field-experienced preconstruction and construction consulting.
