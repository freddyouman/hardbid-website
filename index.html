Our Services

Practical Preconstruction. Reliable Review Support.

Hands-on support that helps contractors clarify scope, organize take-off information, document assumptions, and review possible risk before submitting or executing work.

📋

Bid Package Review

Review of plans, specifications, addenda, bid forms, and scope information for possible missing or unclear requirements.

For contractor review and decision support only. Not a guarantee that all requirements or risks have been identified.
🔍

Scope Gap Analysis

Organize possible gaps, conflicts, and unclear responsibilities between drawings, specs, scopes, and vendor information.

Possible gaps must be verified against full contract documents, addenda, and project-specific conditions.
🧮

Estimating Support

Practical estimating-support organization for quantities, assumptions, exclusions, bid notes, and summary preparation.

Final pricing, markups, labor rates, vendor quotes, and bid submissions remain the contractor/client's responsibility.
📊

Take-Off Summaries

Clear quantity take-off summaries organized for estimating and bid planning, based on documents provided.

Quantities must be verified before bid submission, purchasing, fabrication, or contract use.
💧

Water & Wastewater Consulting

Practical construction-document review support for industrial, utility, water, and wastewater-related projects.

Not licensed engineering, design, permitting, operator-certification, or code-compliance services.
📝

Documentation Support

Organize notes, backup, quantities, photos, scope assumptions, and contractor-review documentation.

Not legal claim advice, entitlement determination, engineering directive, or guaranteed change-order recovery.
